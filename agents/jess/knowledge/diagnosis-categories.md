# Diagnosis Categories — Jess's Target List

One strong influencer per category minimum before April 2026 launch.

---

## Active Categories

### Congenital Heart Disease (CHD)
- Community: tight-knit, high emotional engagement, lots of milestone posts
- Key hashtags: #chdawareness #chdwarrior #chdmom #heartwarrior #congenitalheartdefect #heartmom
- What good looks like: parents documenting surgeries, cath labs, milestone moments

### Type 1 Diabetes (T1D)
- Community: very active, lots of practical content (CGM, pump, carb counting), parent-to-parent advice
- Key hashtags: #t1dmom #type1diabetes #t1dwarrior #diabetesmom #t1dlookslikeme #cgmlife
- What good looks like: 4am blood sugar checks, school advocacy, diagnosis anniversaries

### Cerebral Palsy (CP)
- Community: therapy-focused, milestone-driven, strong advocacy voice
- Key hashtags: #cerebralpalsymom #cpmom #cerebralpalsyawareness #cpwarrior #hemiplegia
- What good looks like: PT/OT/ST progress videos, first steps, adaptive equipment

### Down Syndrome
- Community: joyful, celebratory tone, strong inclusion advocacy
- Key hashtags: #downsyndromemom #t21 #trisomy21 #downsyndromelove #dsawareness
- What good looks like: school inclusion posts, therapy wins, daily life

### Autism
- Community: large, diverse — ranges from advocacy to daily life to meltdown reality
- Key hashtags: #autismmom #autismparent #actuallyautistic #autismawareness #sensoryprocessing
- What good looks like: authentic daily life, not inspiration porn, real struggles + wins

### Rare / Undiagnosed Disease
- Community: small, extremely tight, high emotional engagement
- Key hashtags: #raredisease #rarediseasemom #undiagnosed #rarediseasewarrior #zebra
- What good looks like: diagnostic odyssey posts, community building, awareness campaigns

### Feeding Issues / G-Tube
- Community: medical complexity overlap, therapy-focused
- Key hashtags: #gtubemom #feedingtherapy #feedingdisorder #gtube #feedingaversion
- What good looks like: food chaining progress, g-tube life, feeding therapy wins

### Epilepsy / Seizure Disorders
- Community: high anxiety around safety, strong advocacy
- Key hashtags: #epilepsymom #seizurewarrior #epilepsyawareness #dravet #infantilespasms
- What good looks like: seizure diary content, medication journey, seizure-free milestones

### Premature Birth / NICU
- Community: NICU graduation content, adjusted age milestones, PTSD/anxiety posts
- Key hashtags: #nicumom #prematurebaby #micropreemie #nicugraduate #preemiestrong
- What good looks like: NICU anniversary posts, adjusted age celebrations, honest PTSD content

### Cystic Fibrosis (CF)
- Community: treatment-focused (Trikafta), very tight community
- Key hashtags: #cysticfibrosis #cfmom #cfwarrior #trikafta #65roses
- What good looks like: treatment wins, lung function updates, CF awareness

## Notes on Discovery

- Always start with 2–3 hashtags per category
- Pull 30–50 accounts per hashtag, filter for follower range, then score
- If a category hashtag returns thin results, try broader terms (#heartmom instead of #chdmom)
- Some categories overlap — an account can appear under multiple hashtags, dedup by handle
