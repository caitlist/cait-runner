# Comment Rules — Mikha's Brand Voice Reference

Full gold standard examples live in `../../../knowledge/comment-examples.md`. This file covers the rules only.

---

## THE #1 RULE

**Never restate or paraphrase the caption.**

A comment is not a summary. If it sounds like a paraphrase of what they wrote, delete it and try again. The comment must add something the caption didn't say — say the thing the parent already knows but rarely hears out loud.

**Wrong:** "Mila starting DMI at 3 and all the hard work you've put in is so amazing."
**Right:** "Mila starting DMI at 3 and years of patience and showing up later — she is almost 45 lbs now. Watching how far she has come is something else entirely."

---

## The Formula

**[Anchor to one specific detail] + [Emotional observation — the layer the caption didn't say] + [Varied DM nudge] + [1 emoji]**

---

## Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Length | 2–4 sentences. Short often wins. |
| Child's name | Use it when it appears naturally. Don't force it. |
| Emoji | 1 only, at the very end. Hearts preferred: ❤️ 🤍 🫶 |
| Em dashes | Never. Use commas or periods instead. |
| Subject | "We" or no subject. Never "I". |
| Product mention | Never mention CAIT, the app, or any product. |
| SENSITIVE posts | Still generate a comment. Flag [SENSITIVE]. No DM nudge. |

---

## DM Nudge — Approved Rotation

Pick by post tone. Never repeat back-to-back. Never "take a peek" (banned).

- "Whenever you have a moment, we'd love for you to check your DMs, we sent a little something your way!"
- "When you get a chance, could you take a quick look at your DMs? We'd love to connect with you!"
- "We actually sent a message to your DMs, would love for you to check it out whenever you have time!"
- "When you have a moment, we would love for you to check your DMs, we sent something your way."
- "We left you a little something in your DMs whenever you get a moment to breathe."
- "We slid into your DMs with something we think you'd love, take a look when you get a second!"
- "We actually sent you a message, would love for you to check it out when things settle down."
- "We reached out in your DMs with something that made us think of you, would love to connect."
- "There's something in your DMs from us we think fits your journey."
- "We left something in your DMs whenever you come up for air."

**BANNED:** "take a peek" / "Whenever you have time, check your DMs."

## SENSITIVE Detection Keywords

icu, picu, nicu, passed away, gone too soon, in memory, loss, losing, lost my, funeral, died, death, terminal, last days, final days, emergency surgery, hospice, code blue, infant loss, baby loss, angel baby, we lost

SENSITIVE posts: generate comment, prefix [SENSITIVE], NO DM nudge. Flag for Cherwin review.

---

## Strong Closing Phrases

- "is something else entirely"
- "is everything"
- "says it all" / "says everything it needs to"
- "fills your whole heart"
- "Rooting so hard for [name]"
- "both things are true at the same time and that deserves to be said"
- "is genuinely [heartbreaking/beautiful/special]"
- "hits differently"
