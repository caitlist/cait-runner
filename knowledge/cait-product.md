# CAIT Product Knowledge

## What CAIT Is

CAIT is an AI-powered support tool for caregiving families. Think of it as a specialized AI companion — like ChatGPT but built exclusively for parents managing a child's health, therapy, and daily care.

**Core capabilities:**
- Tracks symptoms, medications, therapy notes, behavioral patterns, sleep, and stress over time
- Generates clinician-ready PDF summaries families can bring to every appointment
- Organizes everything into AI-powered Smart Folders that automatically categorize information
- Provides context-aware personalized guidance that learns and improves the more a family uses it
- HIPAA-protected — the CAIT team cannot see user conversations

**What makes it different from generic AI:**
- It knows the child's full history across every session
- It's built for the specific language and context of medical parenting (IEPs, therapy notes, symptom tracking)
- It generates documents that clinicians actually find useful
- It reduces the mental load of coordinating care across multiple providers

## Value Proposition by Audience Segment

Use this when determining partnership angles and enrichment notes.

### BCBAs (Board Certified Behavior Analysts)
**Their audience:** Parents of autistic children who trust them and follow their recommendations.
**Why CAIT:** Their clients' families are overwhelmed with data — ABA session notes, behavior logs, school reports. CAIT organizes it all and generates summaries they can share with the BCBA directly. CAIT reduces the admin burden on the family, which means better engagement with ABA therapy.
**Angle:** "Your clients' families will have better-organized information for every session — and you'll spend less time gathering history."

### OTs and Speech Therapists (SLPs)
**Their audience:** Parents managing complex therapy schedules across multiple providers.
**Why CAIT:** Therapy notes, home program logs, progress photos — CAIT organizes all of it and generates clinician-ready summaries. Therapists get better-informed parents. Parents feel more in control.
**Angle:** "Parents who use CAIT show up to therapy sessions with organized progress notes, not scattered screenshots."

### Pediatric Doctors and Nurses
**Their audience:** Medical families who trust their medical guidance completely.
**Why CAIT:** The PDF summary feature is the clearest use case — a parent walks into an appointment with a clean, organized health summary instead of a pile of notes. CAIT is a clinical communication tool, not just a journaling app.
**Angle:** "Imagine if every patient family showed up with a structured health timeline instead of a phone full of screenshots."

### Educators and Course Sellers (Parent-Focused Courses)
**Their audience:** Parents actively seeking tools and education for their child's condition.
**Why CAIT:** Their audience is already buying solutions. CAIT is the tool that works alongside what they teach. Course creators can position CAIT as a companion app to their curriculum — tracking progress, journaling insights from the course, generating summaries for the family's care team.
**Angle:** "Your students need a place to apply what they're learning — CAIT is where their action plans, notes, and progress live."

### Journey Accounts (Medical Mom/Dad Accounts)
**These ARE CAIT's users.** They are not partners — they are the audience. Their followers are CAIT's community.
**Why they matter:** 50K followers of an autism journey account = 50K parents who are exactly who CAIT is built for.
**Angle for outreach:** Show them CAIT as a tool for their own family, then ask them to share it with their community.

### Foundations and Advocacy Organizations
**Their audience:** The entire caregiving community for a specific diagnosis.
**Why CAIT:** Foundations want to provide real value to their community. CAIT is a free tool (at launch) that helps the exact families they serve.
**Angle:** "We want to make CAIT available to the families in your community — can we partner on a launch announcement?"

## CAIT's Community Feature

CAIT has an in-app community feature where the CAIT team controls the algorithm and decides which expert profiles to push. BCBAs, OTs, SLPs, and educators can have profiles/storefronts inside the app.

This is the CAIT Community tab's purpose — not just outreach partnerships, but potential in-app partners whose profiles will be visible to every CAIT user.

This makes the CAIT Community tab the highest-value list. Every qualified entry is a potential in-app partner with direct access to CAIT's entire user base.
